About Us

Where did we come from?   What's our backstory?

Back in 2012, when we were deeply entrenched in the industry, we embarked on a tour of various college campuses for hiring events. It became evident that many students were grappling with basic coding problems related to Data Structures and Algorithms. After conducting thorough market research, it was clear that the unemployment rate was soaring at 80%, largely due to inadequate education and an excess of inexperienced graduates in the job market. This revelation sparked a vision, leading us to establish Pyramid Consulting with the goal of bringing quality education to the community and empowering aspiring engineering students to become proficient coders.

In November 2012, Pyramid Consulting began its journey . Fast forward to today, and we take pride in mentoring multiple batches, each consisting of over 100+ students.

But why did we kick-start Pyramid Consulting in the first place? What's our ultimate goal?

Well, it all boils down to the difference between working hard and working smart. At Pyramid Consulting, we offer a unique blend of both approaches to help you become a top-tier coder. It's your one-stop destination to master the art of acing coding interviews at prestigious product-based companies.

Coding, at its core, is about problem-solving. It's about crafting logical, efficient solutions. Here, we're here to nurture and sharpen your analytical and logical thinking skills to perfection. Moreover, we'll connect you with the right companies worldwide based on your skills and preferences. We'll handle all the nitty-gritty details to ensure you land your dream job.

So, what's the grand plan? What's our vision for the future?
We envision a world where every engineering student has access to top-notch education, and every engineering graduate possesses the technical prowess to secure a job. Our goal is to create an ecosystem that equips learners with industry-relevant skills to achieve their dreams and lead a prosperous life.

  • We create and design the content as per the needs, so that they are not just topically relevant however, visually pleasing too.
  • Our exciting and cutting- edge training methodologies bring the essence of “Experiential Learning” at its best.
  • Finally, the key ingredients namely Determination, Dedication and Drive are surely meant to Delight. We are extremely committed to ensuring that experience each time and every time.

Jobs

Job Title:

Trainer for Product-Based and Service-Based Courses

Location:

Across Multiple PAN India Locations

Company:

Pyramid Consulting

About Us:

Pyramid Consulting is a leading provider of high-quality education and training solutions for engineering students. We are dedicated to equipping students with the knowledge and skills they need to excel in the ever-evolving tech industry.

Job Description:

We are seeking experienced and dynamic Trainers to join our team and deliver courses in various product-based and service-based domains to engineering students. As a Trainer at Pyramid Consulting, you will have the opportunity to make a significant impact on students' learning journeys and contribute to their professional development.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Course Delivery: Conduct engaging and informative training sessions on product-based and service-based courses, ensuring students grasp complex concepts effectively.
  • Curriculum Development: Collaborate with the curriculum development team to create and enhance course materials, assignments, and assessments.
  • Student Engagement: Foster a positive and interactive learning environment, encouraging active participation and addressing students' queries and concerns.
  • Assessment and Feedback: Evaluate student performance through regular assessments and provide constructive feedback for improvement.
  • Stay Updated: Keep abreast of industry trends and emerging technologies to ensure course content remains relevant and up-to-date.
  • Quality Assurance: Maintain the highest standards of training quality, consistency, and adherence to educational best practices.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in a relevant field (e.g., Computer Science, Engineering).
  • Proven experience in training or teaching, particularly in product-based and service-based domains.
  • Strong knowledge of the subject matter and a passion for sharing knowledge with students.
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills.
  • Patience and the ability to adapt teaching methods to different learning styles.
  • Problem-solving skills and the ability to simplify complex topics.
  • Continuous learning mindset and commitment to professional development.

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunity to work with motivated and talented engineering students.
  • Access to resources and professional development opportunities.
  • Collaborative and supportive work environment.

How to Apply:

If you are passionate about education and have the skills to inspire the next generation of engineers, we encourage you to apply. Please submit your CV, a cover letter detailing your relevant experience, and any other supporting documents to - sudheer@pyramidconsulting.co.in.

Pyramid Consulting is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive and supportive work environment for all.
